About DRMC

Dhaka Residential Model College (DRMC) is one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Bangladesh. Established in 1960, DRMC has been providing quality education and fostering the holistic development of students for decades. Our mission is to cultivate academic excellence and prepare students for future success in their personal and professional lives.
